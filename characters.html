The "13 reasons why" characters and everything you need to know about them!


  • Hannah: One of the lead characters. She was severely bullied on her previous school, this is why her parents moved and she eventually joined a new but also her last ever school. The bullying continued on her new school, which was one of the reasons she eventually committed. She also got sexually labelled, asssaulted and abused.
  • Bryce: One of the darkest characters in the show, Bryce Walker. He was a real jock, being captain of the football team and pitcher on the baseball team. He also grew up in a wealthy family, but this caused him to be on his own often. He often took advantage of unconcious girls which he also did to Hannah.
  • Clay: He is a 17 year old student at liberty high who lives with his parents. Unfortunately we didn't get to see much of Clay's backstory. He was Hannah's love interest and close friend. He was the only one who received the set of thirteen tapes, without taking a part in the reason of her death
  • Justin: Justin was shown to have a difficult home life. His mother was a neglectful drug addict with a lot of dangerous and violent boyfriends, including Seth, who strangled Justin on screen and was was said to have abused him in the pas. It is also said that one of Jusin's stepfathers molested Justin when he was five.
  • Jessica: One of Hannah's bestfriends before she died. She is a student and the Student Body President at Liberty High School and the ex-girlfriend of Alex Standall and Justin Foley. Her friendship with Hannah ended after they had a misunderstanding and a couple months later Hannah passed away.
  • Tony: Also known as Antonio, is a high school student at Liberty High who helped Clay dealing with Hannah's death. He was also the person who saw Hannah get taken away in an ambulance right after she committed. But most importantly, Hannah gave him the task to make sure the tapes dont get destroyed as they were passed down.
  • Zach: Zach lost his dad in the summer of 2017; he implied to having felt depressed and/or suicidal after his father's death.
